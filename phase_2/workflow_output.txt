EXECUTING STEP 1: 1. Define user personas and user stories for different user types (e.g., email administrators, customer support agents, end-users).
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Result:
User Personas:
1. Email Administrator: Emily, the Email Administrator at a mid-sized company, manages the email routing system to ensure efficient distribution of incoming emails to the relevant departments.
2. Customer Support Agent: Alex, a Customer Support Agent at a tech company, relies on the email router to receive only relevant customer inquiries and provide timely responses.
3. End-User: Sarah, a Sales Representative at a software company, depends on the email router to flag urgent prospect emails for quick follow-ups.

User Stories:
1. Email Administrator
As an Email Administrator, I want to configure routing rules so that incoming emails are automatically distributed to the right teams without manual intervention.

2. Customer Support Agent
As a Customer Support Agent, I want to receive only emails relevant to my expertise so that I can provide better and faster responses to customers.

3. End-User
As an End-User, I want urgent emails from important clients flagged immediately so that I can respond promptly and not miss any critical communication.

====================================================================================================

EXECUTING STEP 2: 2. Based on user stories, define product features such as email account setup, automatic email routing, customizable routing rules, email tracking, and reporting.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Result:
Feature Name: Email Account Setup
Description: Users can easily configure and connect their email accounts to the Email Router system for automated email management.
Key Functionality: 
- Step-by-step setup wizard for adding email accounts
- Support for major email platforms (Gmail, Outlook, Exchange)
- Secure authentication process for account linkage
User Benefit: Simplifies the integration process, allowing users to quickly set up their email accounts for efficient email routing without technical complications.

Feature Name: Automatic Email Routing
Description: Automatically categorize, prioritize, and direct incoming emails to the appropriate departments or team members based on predefined rules.
Key Functionality: 
- AI-powered classification to analyze email content
- Route emails based on predefined categories (Sales, Support, HR, etc.)
- Escalate urgent emails for immediate attention
User Benefit: Saves time and ensures emails are efficiently directed to the right recipients, improving workflow productivity.

Feature Name: Customizable Routing Rules
Description: Users can create and manage personalized routing rules to tailor email distribution based on specific criteria.
Key Functionality: 
- User-defined rules for email categorization and assignment
- Allow users to set priority levels for different types of emails
- Support for skill-based routing for specialized inquiries
User Benefit: Empowers users to customize email routing to align with their unique workflow requirements, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in email management.

Feature Name: Email Tracking
Description: Track and monitor email volume trends, response times, and resolution rates for performance evaluation and optimization.
Key Functionality: 
- Analytics dashboard displaying email volume trends by category
- Track response times and resolution rates for emails
- Monitor team workload distribution
User Benefit: Provides insights into email processing efficiency, enabling users to identify bottlenecks, improve response times, and optimize workload distribution for enhanced productivity.

Feature Name: Reporting
Description: Generate detailed performance reports to measure the success and effectiveness of email routing and management processes.
Key Functionality: 
- Generate performance reports on email routing metrics
- Visualize classification accuracy and response time improvements
- Customizable reporting options for diverse stakeholder needs
User Benefit: Enables users to assess the impact of email routing strategies, track progress towards defined success metrics, and identify areas for further optimization to enhance customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
